Why Milan?

Milan is one of Italy's most exciting cities, combining centuries of history with modern fashion, architecture, and world-class cuisine. Known as the fashion capital of Italy, Milan offers visitors incredible shopping, iconic landmarks, and unforgettable cultural experiences.

From admiring the breathtaking Duomo Cathedral to relaxing in luxurious spas or taking day trips to Lake Como, Milan is the perfect base for discovering northern Italy. The city blends elegant piazzas, hidden trattorias, and vibrant nightlife, making it a destination that offers something for every traveller.

What to do in Milan

Duomo di Milano

The Duomo is Milan’s most iconic landmark and one of the largest Gothic cathedrals in the world. Its incredible marble façade and detailed architecture make it one of the most breathtaking buildings in Italy.

Location: Piazza del Duomo, Milan

Why visit: Climb to the rooftop terraces for panoramic views across the entire city.

QC Terme Milano

QC Terme Milano is a luxury spa located inside historic Spanish walls in the heart of the city. It offers thermal baths, saunas, relaxation rooms, and beautiful outdoor pools.

Location: Piazzale Medaglie d'Oro, Milan

Why visit: The evening spa experience with thermal pools and city views is incredibly relaxing after a day exploring Milan.

Lake Como

Just a short trip from Milan, Lake Como is famous for its crystal-clear water, colourful villages, and stunning alpine scenery. It is one of the most beautiful destinations in northern Italy.

Location: Lake Como, Lombardy, Italy

Why visit: Take a boat ride across the lake and explore charming towns like Bellagio and Varenna.

Milan Public Transport Overview

Milan’s public transport network is efficient, modern, and easy to navigate, connecting the city’s historic landmarks, shopping districts, and cultural hubs. Whether you’re a first-time visitor or a regular commuter, this overview highlights the main metro, tram, and bus lines along with their key stops, making it easier to plan your routes and explore Milan with confidence and convenience. From the iconic M1 Red Line to the scenic tram routes, Milan offers options that are fast, reliable, and accessible, ensuring you can reach the heart of the city as well as hidden gems off the beaten path.
